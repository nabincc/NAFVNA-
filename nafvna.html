NAFVNA DOMINION — The Central Holding Company

NAFVNA DOMINION (ND) is the apex holding company of the Central Group, constituted in 2020 to exercise strategic oversight, capital stewardship, and institutional governance across eleven regulated sectors. Every subsidiary operates under a unified compliance and governance framework established and enforced by the Central Group.
